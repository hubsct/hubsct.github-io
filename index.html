Washing Hands

  • You should wash your hands therally, with soap!

  • Always try to sing "Happy Birthday" 2 times!

  • If you don't have soap, bring gloves and sanitizer!

Wearing Masks

  • Always wear a mask, you made, or an amazon mask!

  • A normal surgical mask, or clone mask will do!

  • Put it on tightly, to cover your nose, and mouth!


Maintaining Distance

  • Try to be 6 feet apart from everyone you are near!

  • Be cautious, even if they are your family members!

  • If you are needed to be closer, keep on your mask!
